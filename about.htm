  About Us
 
   National Hospital (www.nationalhospital.com) is a healthcare
resource intended for patients, healthcare providers & students.
National Hospital provides directories of hospitals, physicians,
associations, government resources, & more. National Hospital
will create a marketplace for telemedical services. The services
will be purchased from physicians and then resold to patients,
companies, schools, prisons, hospitals, and (other) physicians.

   National Hospital is owned & operated by National Hospital
Affiliates, Inc., which is a National Health Information Exchange
composed of a national network of geographically specific online
hospitals. National Hospital will serve as the gateway of a network
of Hospitals from Alabama to Wyoming. Although the Hospitals
function independently, they will work together to fulfill regional
and national information networks.

   National Hospital Affiliates, Inc. was founded in October 2008
by Patrick J. Arieno. Mr. Arieno's healthcare experience includes:
Emergency Room Technician at Point Coupee General Hospital,
Emergency Medical Technician for Acadian Ambulance, & Flow
Cytometry Phenotypying Technologist for the Cancer Center at
the University of New Mexico. Mr. Arieno has a B.S. in Biology
& Chemistry from the University of New Mexico, & an M.B.A. in
Finance and Business & Public Policy from the Simon Graduate
School of Business Administration at the University of Rochester.
